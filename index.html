Description

Semmes Building, a premier commercial office property at 10220 River Road, Potomac, MD 20854 (the “Property”), offers an exceptional opportunity for tenants or investors seeking prestige, visibility, and accessibility in the Potomac corridor. Built in 1979, the Semmes Building is a well‑known multi‑tenant office building with professional suites that cater to legal, consulting, education, and health‑service firms.

Centrally located in Potomac, the Property enjoys high exposure on River Road, one of the area’s major thoroughfares. Suites are well appointed, offering flexible layouts — many with street‑facing windows and convenient building access. Tenants benefit from established neighborhood amenities, ease of travel, and proximity to key markets in Montgomery County and greater DC.

The Property’s income stream is supported by a diversified tenant mix, including law offices, consulting groups, and service providers. There is strong potential upside through lease renewals, modernization of select interiors, and optimizing under‑utilized space.

With room for aesthetic updates, improved signage, and modern finishes, the Semmes Building is positioned to deliver durable value and enhanced returns for the right owner or tenant seeking presence in Potomac’s professional business community.
